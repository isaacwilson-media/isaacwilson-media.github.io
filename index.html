About Me

I'm Isaac Wilson, a passionate game designer and cinematic artist with a deep love for immersive storytelling. Inspired by titles like Red Dead Redemption 2, I'm driven to create worlds that feel alive — where every cut scene, every animation, and every frame tells a story.

I am studying BSc (Hons) Creative Media Technologies at the University of Portsmouth, where I am developing skills in motion capture, 3D CGI, visual effects, and cinematic production using industry-standard tools and facilities.

My ambition is to work as a Cinematic Designer or Mocap Animator at a major game studio, crafting the kind of emotionally resonant cut scenes that stay with players long after they've put the controller down.
